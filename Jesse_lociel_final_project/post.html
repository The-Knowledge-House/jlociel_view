College Is Too Much For Me.

At This point, I Might Just Drop Out.

Posted by Anonymous on October 13, 2010

College is too much for me. I can't keep up with my classes. I just barely make it on time. I don't finish copying all the notes. I'm missing some assignments in most of my classes. And now I have finals coming up soon. I study and study, but it just doesn't help. I've asked my friends for help, but they're all busy.

On top of that, I'm working late everyday just to pay for tuition. I don't get much sleep anymore. I won't pass my finals. I won't pass the classes. I won't graduate. I......I don't know what to do. I don't want to drop out, but at this rate I just might have to. I want to keep trying. I want to get better, but I might not have the chance to do so. I....I'm scared.
